Core Service Lines

Six focused ways we protect people, property, and operations.

Each service can operate alone or as part of a wider protection model depending on your site footprint, occupancy, and risk profile.

Security Guarding

Visible officer presence for daily site reassurance.

Professional guards who support safety, access awareness, and structured incident handling across business and residential environments.

  • Front-of-house and perimeter visibility.
  • Professional site representation.
  • Supervisor-led reporting and escalation.

Monitoring and Screening

Live oversight that strengthens awareness beyond the gate.

Monitoring workflows and thermal support help maintain continuity, detect issues early, and keep response decisions informed.

  • 24/7 watchfulness and escalation support.
  • Thermal screening when required.
  • Faster incident visibility for clients and supervisors.

Access Control

Manage movement through reception points and restricted zones.

We help clients maintain clear entry discipline, reduce unauthorised access, and improve accountability across staff and visitor traffic.

  • Controlled visitor and contractor handling.
  • Reception and gatehouse support.
  • Structured entry logging and escalation.

Event Security

Protect guest experience while keeping the environment controlled.

Our event teams support stewarding, queue management, guest entry, and coordinated incident response for public-facing environments.

  • Crowd-safe entry and movement management.
  • Professional public-facing officers.
  • Structured coordination during peak periods.

Mobile Patrols

Out-of-hours checks, lock routines, and wider footprint coverage.

Patrol-led services help maintain deterrence, secure openings and closures, and add reassurance where full-time static cover is not required.

  • Scheduled and reactive patrol attendance.
  • Lock and unlock support.
  • Perimeter awareness for larger sites.

Door and Specialist Support

Additional cover for venues, high-footfall sites, and higher-risk periods.

We provide door supervision and specialist support options where environments need tighter entry handling or stronger visible deterrence.

  • Door supervision and venue entry control.
  • Canine and specialist deterrence support.
  • Flexible reinforcement for changing conditions.
